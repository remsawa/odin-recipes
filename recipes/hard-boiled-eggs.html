Hard boiled eggs

Description

A tasty and easy way to eat eggs.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Water
  • Cold water

Steps

  1. Take your eggs out of the refrigerator
  2. After you taken your eggs out, filled a pot with water and heat on medium heat until comes to a boil.
  3. Once the water is boiling gently place your eggs in the water with a laddle or spoon
  4. Depending on how well you want your yolks cooked. Boil your eggs for 6 to 12 minutes.
  5. Prepare a bowl cold water (even better if it's ice water).
  6. Turn of the heat and gently take you eggs out of the hot and place them in the cold water
  7. Let the eggd chill in the cold water for around five minutes until they've cooled down completly.
  8. Take your eggs out of the cold water and carefully peel the shells of the eggs off.
