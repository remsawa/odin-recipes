Grilled cheese

Description

A tasty melted cheese sandwhich.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Sliced bread
  • American cheese (or your favorite meltable cheese)

Steps

  1. Heat up a pan on low-medium heat.
  2. Once your pan has warmed up, butter the pan by adding a teaspoon of butter.
  3. On a plate assemble your sandwhich, putting your cheese in between two slices of bread.
  4. Add your sandwhich to the pan and let cook until the bread in a golden brown, then flip the it over and cook the other side.
  5. Cook until the cheese has melted and the bread is golden brown.
