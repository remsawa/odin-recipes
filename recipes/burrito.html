Burrito

Description

A delicious mexican dish encased within a soft tortilla. Burritos can contain a variety ingredients such as beans, rice, meat, cheese, and salsa.

Ingredients

  • Cooking oil
  • Ground beef
  • Taco seasoning
  • Rice
  • Lettuce
  • Cheese
  • Onion
  • Beans
  • Garlic
  • Diced tomatos
  • Tortilla

Steps

  1. Warm the oil in a skillet over high heat. Add the garlic and onion, cooking for about 2 minutes until the onion begins to brown. Stir in the beef, breaking it into pieces as it cooks, until it changes from red to brown.
  2. Add taco seasoning and mix with the beef. Cook until most of the water have evaporated.
  3. Warm up your tortillas in a pan on low to medium heat for a few seconds to soften them.
  4. Place the meat, diced tomatos, beans, cheese, rice, and lettuce on top of your tortilla. Beware of overstuffing as it may rip your tortilla.
  5. Wrap your burrito up by first folding the tortillas bottom over the filling, then fold it's sides in the same manner tightly.
